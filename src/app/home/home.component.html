Features

NRC Standards

All 14 animal species, 450 sub species, conform to NRC Standards.

Extensive Feedstuffs Database

Our Database contains over 260 Feedstuff with values for all 60 Nutrients.

Linear-Programming Procedure

Formulations are based on a least-cost linear programming procedure.

Options

Standard Formulation Option

In the Standard Formulations Option, formulations are performed up to the macro-nutrients stage with no limit place on the number of formulations, and are free of charge. The composition of the formulated ration is set out in table form, together with the cost per ton, which of course excludes any mixing and/or blending costs.

Extended Formulations Option

Extended Formulations Option is an extension to our Standard Formulations Option and offers to its users invaluable addtional features.

These features inlcudes:

  • Recieving nutrient statuses of the formulated rations, by listing all nutrients values and labelling those that are inadequate supply.
  • Recieving a customized micro-nutrient supplement mix, which, when added to the 1 ton mix, will rectify any deficiencies found in the initial macro formulation.
  • The option to add your own feedstuffs with their nutrient content as well as any other product as an ingredient.
